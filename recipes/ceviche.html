Ceviche

Description

Ceviche is a very light dish that you eat on tostadas or with corn chips. It's great for seafood lovers.

Mixed with shrimp and crab meat, ceviche is the perfect dish for the family. You can make it as spicy as you want and add any kind of vegetable to it.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Cooked boiled shrimp
  • 1 lb Crav meat or imitation crab meat
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 4 serrano peppers
  • 1 bushel of cilantro
  • 2 limes
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • Salt to taste
  • A bottle of mayonnaise
  • One bag of tostadas

Steps

  1. Chop the cooked boiled shrimp.
  2. Mix with the crab meat in a big container.
  3. Dice the onions, tomatoes, peppers and cilantro.
  4. Add them to the meat and stir everything up. Then add salt, the limes.
  5. You can optionally add cucumbers or any other vegetable to taste.
  6. Serve the ceviche on a tostada over a bed of mayonnaise.
