Shrimp Alfredo

Description

The shrimpo alfredo is a very creamy and delicious meal that you can make to serve many people. The noodles allow you to feed many people leaving them happy and satisfied.

This meal is usually paired with bread of your choice but we will not include it in the ingredients. You can decide how creamy you want it to be.

Ingredients

  • Shrimp 5lbs
  • 2 cups Heavy Cream
  • 1 cup Half and Half
  • 1 cup bag of Parmasan cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 lb of fettuccine
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Boil the fettuccine noodles in water with salt and butter.
  2. Add a little bit of oil with the chopped garlic clove to a frying pan.
  3. Add heavey cream to cooked shrimp and bring to a boil.
  4. Add half and half and also bring to a boil.
  5. Now add all of the parmasan cheese to the boiling half and half and heavy cream.
  6. Stir until the mix is thick enough then add the noodles.
  7. Add the parsley at the end to each plate you serve.
